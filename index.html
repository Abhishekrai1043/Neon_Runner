🌌 ABOUT THE GAME & DEVELOPER

🚀 My First Leap Into Game Dev

Welcome to NeonRunner! This game marks a massive milestone for me—it is my very first official game project. Building this was an incredible journey of combining core software engineering principles, long nights of hard work, and dedicated time with cutting-edge AI development tools.

By leveraging AI as a powerful collaborative partner, I was able to accelerate my development workflow, refine complex physics math, and bring this fast-paced, cross-platform arcade experience to life exactly the way I envisioned it.

👨💻 About Me

I am a passionate developer deeply interested in the intersections of Web Development, AI Engineering, and Interactive Design. I love building systems that are highly optimized, responsive, and genuinely fun to interact with. This project represents my dedication to mastering fluid physics engine mechanics, responsive multi-platform layout design, and clean, future-proof architecture.
